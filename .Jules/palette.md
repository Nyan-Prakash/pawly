## 2025-05-15 - [Standardizing Interactive Feedback and Accessibility]
**Learning:** Icon-only buttons frequently lacked tactile feedback and screen reader labels. Standardizing these through a shared `IconButton` component with built-in spring animations and mandatory-ready accessibility props ensures a consistent, premium feel and baseline accessibility across the entire app.
**Action:** Prefer the enhanced `IconButton` over raw `Pressable` or `TouchableOpacity` for all icon-only interactions to maintain high UX standards automatically.
